# VenturePack MVP Specification Summary

VenturePack is a secure pre-counsel preparation platform for startup founders.

It is not:
- a law firm
- an attorney
- an AI lawyer
- an AI paralegal
- legal advice
- a replacement for licensed counsel
- a legal compliance certification

The product helps founders:
- organize company information
- complete structured onboarding
- create a persistent company profile
- track preparation completion
- create matters
- generate counsel preparation packets
- request attorney matching

The MVP must not include:
- contract risk review
- clause red flag detection
- execution-ready legal drafting
- securities document generation
- legal compliance certification
- AI lawyer language
- attorney-quality claims

Use these phrases:
- pre-counsel preparation
- startup preparation
- preparation workspace
- preparation completion
- counsel packet
- questions for counsel

Do not use:
- legally ready
- compliant
- guaranteed
- replace your lawyer
- AI lawyer
- legal advice

Core MVP pages:
1. Home page
2. App dashboard
3. Guided onboarding
4. Company profile
5. Matters
6. Counsel packet
7. Attorney matching request
8. Admin mock console

Preparation categories:
- Company structure
- Founder alignment
- Ownership information
- Intellectual property
- Contractors and employees
- Customer activity
- Privacy and data practices
- Fundraising preparation
- Corporate records
- Counsel readiness

The preparation score is only a completion percentage. It is not a legal risk score, compliance score, or legal opinion.

Counsel packet sections:
- Cover page
- Company overview
- Matter summary
- People involved
- Document inventory
- Open questions
- Source and generation information
- Disclaimer that packet is not a legal opinion

The MVP succeeds if founders complete the process, understand their company more clearly, and arrive at counsel with a better organized matter.
